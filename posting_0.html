\n Cititec has partnered with a B2C scale up to support them on their mission to find a Data Engineer. Having started almost ten years ago, they have been growing rapidly, particularly their data function.\n

\n

\n As a Data Engineer you will play a crucial part in building out their customer data platform. This platform is crucial to the whole business and therefore so is the data team. It is crucial because the data coming in from the platform is used to for the company\xe2\x80\x99s marketing campaigns and events.\n

\n

\n This is a great opportunity for a data engineer who wants to work on exciting greenfield projects in an autonomous working environment. In addition, you will get exposure across the business, playing a key part in helping senior stake-holders to make crucial decisions about the company\xe2\x80\x99s future.\n

\n

\n \n \n What you\xe2\x80\x99ll have the opportunity to do as a Data Engineer:\n \n \n

\n
    \n
  • \n Integrate external tools and databases to the customer data platform\n
    • \n
  • \n Working closely with the Head of Data to configure the marketing databases, marketing and track performance\n
    • \n
  • \n Building, maintaining and monitoring data pipelines\n
    • \n
  • \n Working with key stakeholders across the business\n
