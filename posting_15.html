\n \n \n What We Do\n
\n
\n \n \n Time is running out.\n
\n
\n For Europe to have a fighting chance to reach its net zero targets, we need to transform our entire economy within an unprecedented scale and timeframe. The EU is leveraging sustainable finance regulations as the catalyst that will drive this transformation. We are building the technology that will scale their adoption.\n
\n
\n Our SaaS platform will harness the scale of data and artificial intelligence to help companies navigate and implement these regulatory frameworks, removing the significant cost and time barriers that are blocking them from achieving their climate neutrality objectives.\n
\n
\n Based out of the Berlin AI Campus and financially backed by Merantix AG, we have access to a network of more than 90 engineers, scientists, and entrepreneurs from leading companies and universities. You will be joining an experienced founding team with backgrounds in machine learning, law & sustainability as well as previous venture and consulting experience in the LegalTech, RegTech and NLP space.\n
\n
\n \n Your role\n
\n
\n \n Joining as our Senior Software Data Engineer\xe2\x80\xa6\n
\n
    \n
  • \n You will shape the core product and it\'s tech stack from the ground up.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will participate in the overall design, architecture, and integration of our data systems.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will build, maintain and run secure cloud-native data collection, ETL and analytics pipelines.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will provide APIs to build user-facing applications on top of our data backends.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will plan, gather requirements, collaborate and communicate with stakeholders.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will be a key part of the technical decision-making process.\n
    • \n
  • \n You will mentor other engineers and strive for best practices.\n
    • \n
  • \n Ultimately, you will use data to help accelerate our mission impact and make a real difference.\n
    \n
    • \n
\n \n If you think you are right for this role but don\xe2\x80\x99t meet all of the requirements, we strongly encourage you to apply anyway. Even if this isn\xe2\x80\x99t a perfect fit, strong candidates will be kept in our talent pool for future hires.\n
\n
