\n \n Jobbeschreibung\n
\n
\n \n We as\n \n Platform Operations\n \n provide infrastructure solutions, consulting and developing in the AWS cloud, including best practices for cloud migration. We collaborate with our internal development teams to migrate existing products to get the most convenient flexibility and the best-of-breed technology running in AWS.\n
\n
\n You are\n \n Cloud Native\n \n and want to build solutions on the cloud infrastructure of AWS? Does your heart beat for\n \n C\n \n \n ool Solutions & Architecture\n \n in\n \n AWS\n \n ?\n
\n
\n You are looking to work in a\n \n L\n \n earning Organization, and you consider error culture as value-creating? You are enthusiastic about working with Agile Methodologies?\n
\n
\n Become part of idealos Platform Operations and join our journey migrating from the data center to cloud-native solution in AWS Cloud!\n
\n
\n The position of\n \n Cloud Engineer (m|f|x) - Platform Operations\n \n is available as a full-time or part-time position.\n

    • \n

  • \n You are passionate and curious about next-generation developments in the cloud\n
    • \n

\n \n
    \n

  • \n You are solution-oriented, apply your communicative skills and interpret your activities as the supply of services.\n
    • \n
  • \n You bring extensive know-how about Terraform, AWS CDK and AWS CloudFormation\n
    • \n
  • \n You bring extensive know-how in at least one of the following areas: AWS Security, AWS ECS & EKS; Lambda, API Gateway, Route53; AWS database specialties like RDS, Aurora, DynamoDB and more; AWS costs optimization and monitoring.\n
