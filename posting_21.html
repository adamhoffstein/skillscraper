\n As a Senior Data Engineer in one of the ZMS data teams you collaborate with data scientists, business analysts, product managers and other engineers to help us prepare ZMS for future scale. Here you can develop and improve our advertising services and data products, contribute to our ML platform on real time auctioning and bidding, design and create our next generation analytics platform, you can help bring our audience building platform to the next level and you can drive new features such as real-time segmentation or audience forecasts.\n
\n
\n The technical scope covers large-scale batch processing pipelines, data modeling, Data Warehouses, ETL, data analysis, stream processing and distributed low-latency databases. You will be using state of the art tools.\n
\n
\n \n WHERE YOUR EXPERTISE IS NEEDED\n
\n
\n \n
    \n
  • \n Provide critical data infrastructures like large-scale ETL processes, DWHs, stream processing and batch processing pipelines, large-scale high-throughput/low-latency data stores, data governance mechanisms\n
    • \n
  • \n Improve the pace of innovation by building and improving ML infrastructures such as training/evaluation frameworks, experimentation frameworks\n
    • \n
  • \n Collaborate with brilliant Product Managers, Data Scientists, Engineers and Analysts across Zalando to create positive customer impact together\n
    • \n
  • \n Proactively provide solutions to problems and drive the implementation\n
    • \n
  • \n Design, implement and run robust solutions for future requirements\n
    • \n
  • \n Raise the technical excellence of our team to the next level with clean code and thoughtful pull request reviews\n
    \n
    \n
    • \n
\n \n \n What We\xe2\x80\x99re Looking For\n
\n
\n \n \n
    \n
  • \n Solid software engineering background\n
    • \n
  • \n Proficient in Python or at least one of the JVM-based languages\n
    • \n
  • \n A few good years of experience in large-scale data engineering using open source technologies and cloud service providers like AWS or GCP\n
    • \n
  • \n Proficient in SQL and database design, including distributed systems\n
    • \n
  • \n Solid experience with large-scale batch processing and stream processing (e.g. Spark, Hadoop, Flink, Storm, Apache Beam, Kafka, Airflow, Oozie etc.)\n
