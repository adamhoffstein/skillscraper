\n Small business owners are at the heart of all we do, so we\'re creating tools that help them run their businesses. With a founder\xe2\x80\x99s mentality and a \'team-first\xe2\x80\x99 attitude, our diverse teams across Europe, South America and the United States work together to ensure that the merchants we partner with can be successful doing what they love.\n
\n
\n As a Data Analyst, you will be part of the Data Intelligence team who focuses on steering SumUps overarching business strategy. This is a chance to work with senior management throughout the business to create impact globally and enable SumUppers to be even more data-informed!\n
\n
\n You\xe2\x80\x99ll have high visibility across all levels of the business, improving standardisation of reporting strategy and data visualisation.\n
\n
\n If you have a passion for finding insights in data that deliver real business value, then this Data Analyst opportunity could be for you!\n
\n
\n \n What you\xe2\x80\x99ll do;\n
\n \n
    \n
  • \n Deliver accurate and reliable data reporting and analysis to steer overall business performance\n
    • \n
  • \n Enhance SumUp\'s global business reports (e.g. improving and optimising dashboard usability and visualization)\n
    • \n
  • \n Regularly assess and monitor data quality of global business KPIs\n
    • \n
  • \n Work with data engineering team to resolve data & reporting issues\n
    • \n
  • \n Enhance SumUp\'s standard business reports, including dashboard design and development, improving usability and visualization, optimizing configuration, and ongoing maintenance\n
    \n
    • \n
\n \n You\xe2\x80\x99ll be great for this role if you have;\n
\n \n
    \n
  • \n 3+ years in an analytical role (ideally a data or BI Analyst focused role)\n
    • \n
  • \n Advanced SQL skills, have used SQL on a regular basis and is able to deliver insights & analysis from complex data sources\n
    • \n
  • \n Extensive experience working with Tableau (dashboard design and development). Other data visualization experience (e.g. Looker, Google DataStudio) is a strong plus.\n
    • \n
  • \n Demonstrable experience in translating data analysis into insights & recommendations for business partners.\n
    • \n
  • \n Strong communication skills. Comfortable working with stakeholders across all levels and enjoy stakeholder enablement.\n
    • \n
  • \n Familiarity with data governance and GDPR.\n
    \n
    • \n
\n \n Why you should join SumUp\n
\n \n
    \n
  • \n You will be part of a global analytics community, with opportunities for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and ongoing training & development.\n
    • \n
  • \n Entitlement to an L&D budget for attending conferences and external training.\n
    • \n
  • \n We offer a corporate pension scheme, 28 days\xe2\x80\x99 paid leave, free German and yoga classes, subsidised Urban Sports Club membership and many other great benefits.\n
